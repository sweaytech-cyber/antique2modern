Frequently asked questions about our services

How often does your inventory change?

Our inventory changes weekly as we process new estate acquisitions throughout southeastern Wisconsin. We recommend visiting often as our best pieces sell quickly!

Do you purchase individual items?

Yes! While most of our inventory comes from complete estate liquidations, we do consider individual pieces. Contact us for a free estimate.

What areas do you serve for estate sales?

We serve the greater Milwaukee area including New Berlin, Waukesha, Brookfield, and surrounding communities.

What types of items do you sell?

We carry everything from 1700s-era antiques to contemporary furniture, including art, home décor, lamps, collectibles, and more.
