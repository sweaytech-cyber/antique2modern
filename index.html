Who We Are

Antique 2 Modern is a local estate sale company that helps families and individuals downsize and liquidate their estates. Our store offers gently used furniture and furnishings collected from these estates. Our inventory changes weekly, so customers are encouraged to visit often.

What We Sell:

  • 1700s-era antiques
  • Vintage furniture
  • Modern and contemporary furniture
  • Artwork and home décor
  • Lighting and unique collectibles
