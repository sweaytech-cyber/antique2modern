About Antique2Modern

We are a family owned business located in the Menomonee Falls area right outside of Milwaukee, Wisconsin. This is a fulltime commitment for us, so we are at your service 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

My wife (Lori) and I have a passion for preserving history and enjoy meeting people and talking about their treasures. Not to mention our genuine love of all things old and antique.

Our History

We've lived in the Milwaukee area all our life and I graduated from the University of Wisconsin – Milwaukee with a BS in Business Marketing and a Masters in eCommerce. I worked as a market analyst for a large medical company for 10 years before committing to Antique2Modern fulltime.
